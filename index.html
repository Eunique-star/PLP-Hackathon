About Me

Hello! I'm an aspiring software developer with a background in Computer Science and a passion for creating meaningful digital solutions. Currently, I teach computer skills to beginners, helping them navigate tools like Microsoft Office and build confidence in using technology.

I'm still growing my skills in software development, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS and a touch of JavaScript. I enjoy transforming my ideas into reality through clean and creative web projects. Beyond coding, I love to write and explore creative ways to tell stories through technology. Every project I work on is a step toward mastering my craft and sharing my passion for innovation with the world.
